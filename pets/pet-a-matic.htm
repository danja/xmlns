Introduction

Pet-a-matic is a simple Javascript application that allows you to create a Pet Profile for your animals. It's FOAF (friend-of-a-friend) for felines (and canines). You can read more about FOAF in Edd Dumbill's "XML Watch: Finding friends with XML and RDF" article, at the FOAF homepage on RDFWeb, and also the FOAF vocabulary description.

The Pet-a-Matic is being provided as a quick and easy way for you to create your own Pet Profiles. Simply work through the forms on this page and complete whichever details you'd like to add to your description. As a minimum you'll need to supply your pet's name and your email address.

Note: none of the information you enter in this page is used or stored in any way. The processing is entirely client-side, so your privacy is assured. Don't forget to save the results!

If you have comments about this application, or further questions about FOAF, why not join the RDFWeb-dev mailing list?
